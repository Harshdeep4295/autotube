**Critical Finding:** YouTube treats Shorts and long-form with fully separate algorithms.

```
Impact on Main Channel Views:
  No suppression detected
  Slight cannibalization: 5-8% (offset by +120% shorts views)
  
Algorithm Independence:
  Shorts views ≠ counted toward watch time threshold
  Shorts RPM = 30-50% of main video RPM
  BUT: Shorts drive subscribers to channel (funnel effect)
```
